About Me

I am a student in the informatics study program at Udayana University. I live in Tista Village, Kerambitan District, Tabanan Regency, Bali. I have several interests which I will explain as below.

I'm a Bali-based photographer passionate about capturing the island's beauty and cultural richness. Through my lens, I aim to showcase the unique moments, scenic landscapes, and vibrant traditions that make Bali special. Each photo is a celebration of the island's charm, shared with the world through my artistic vision.

I am a videographer based in Bali, and my mission is to depict the beauty and cultural richness of this island through visual storytelling. Through my camera and videography skills, each project becomes a creative offering aimed at documenting special moments, delivering memorable visual experiences, and celebrating the rich cultural heritage in every corner of Bali.

I am a designer with a focus on UI/UX design, based in Bali. My dedication lies in creating engaging and intuitive design experiences. Through graphic design and UI/UX, I strive to deliver innovative and functional aesthetic solutions. With my imagination and design skills, I am committed to creating captivating interfaces, enhancing user experiences, and integrating local elements to add value to each of my design projects.

I am a software developer specializing in web and desktop applications. Based in Bali, I am passionate about crafting efficient and user-friendly software solutions. My expertise lies in creating robust web applications and desktop software, incorporating cutting-edge technologies to deliver seamless and impactful user experiences. With a commitment to excellence in coding and problem-solving, I strive to contribute innovative solutions to the world of software development.
