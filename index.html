About Me

I am a student in the informatics study program at Udayana University. I live in Tista Village, Kerambitan District, Tabanan Regency, Bali. I have several interests which I will explain as below.

I am a photographer based in Bali, an island renowned for its natural beauty and rich culture. With my camera as a steadfast companion, I strive to capture the beauty and uniqueness of every moment, blending technical expertise in photography with a profound appreciation for Bali's natural wonders and cultural richness. Through my lens, I aim to celebrate the island's scenic landscapes, immortalize the beauty of local traditions and arts, and narrate profound stories from a visual perspective. Every photo I take is an endeavor to portray the splendor and marvel that make Bali so special, and I hope to share it with the world through the art of photography.
